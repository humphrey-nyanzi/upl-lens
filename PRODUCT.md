# Product

## Register

product

## Users
UPL Lens serves stats-curious Uganda Premier League fans first, with secondary use by analysts, journalists, and technical reviewers. Users usually arrive to understand league patterns quickly, then drill deeper into team, match, player, and season trends.

## Product Purpose
UPL Lens turns official UPL match data into trustworthy football intelligence. Success means users can discover meaningful league insights that are not obvious from fixtures and results pages, while trusting caveats, freshness signals, and transparent methodology.

## Brand Personality
Neutral, credible, analytical. The voice should feel calm and evidence-led: clear enough for everyday fans, serious enough for analysts, and honest about data limits.

## Anti-references
- Generic admin-template dashboards with no football story.
- Marketing-first landing pages that hide the product workflow.
- Visual styles that prioritize decoration over readability and data trust.
- Interfaces that mimic official fixtures pages instead of adding insight.

## Design Principles
- Insight first: every screen should surface football meaning before raw numbers.
- Trust by default: show caveats, freshness, and uncertainty visibly when relevant.
- Familiar task flow: keep navigation and controls predictable so analysis is easy to continue.
- Progressive depth: provide quick summaries first, then clear drill-down paths.
- Product over portfolio: demonstrate technical quality through reliability and clarity, not self-promotion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA as a baseline. Keep readable contrast, clear focus states, keyboard-accessible interactions, and reduced-motion support. Prioritize mobile readability and touch-friendly controls for broad device access.
